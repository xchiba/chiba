How do you make your music?

There is no fixed method that i use to make songs, it is a bit like it happens at the moment, sometimes i use Caustic 3 (most of the time) but then i mix everything with audacity, i use vocaloid 5 for the vocals and then i add something with FL Studio.
I can say though that most of the time the main DAW is Caustic 3.
Sometimes when i don't feel like composing, i record my instruments in multitrack on the phone.

What instruments do you use?

I use an electric guitar, a digital keyboard, a bass, and i make the drums on Caustic 3.

My Guitar is a Squier Jaguar, my Bass is an Ibanez gsr180 and my Keyboard is a Yamaha EZ-220
